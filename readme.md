# RankoneQoL - Intelligent Suite for Classic & SoD

**RankoneQoL** is a lightweight, high-performance quality-of-life suite designed to strip away the tedious micro-management of World of Warcraft. Wrapped in a modern, flat sci-fi user interface, it accelerates your world progression, fully automates merchant dealings, and synchronizes your game tooltips under a stunning, real-time theme engine.

---

## ⚡ Core Features

### 🚀 World Travel & Speedrun Acceleration
* **Instant Hearthstone Binder:** Bypasses and completely auto-confirms the annoying confirmation popup when setting a new home location at an innkeeper.
* **Smart Flightmaster Skip:** Instantly forces open the flight map the millisecond you click a flightmaster, unless they have active quest dialogs for you.
* **Gossip Auto-Skipper:** Automatically processes and clicks through trivial single-choice NPC greetings to save valuable seconds.

### ⚙️ Full Interaction Automation
* **Junk Vendor Engine:** Swiftly empties your bags of useless poor-quality (grey) items as soon as you open any vendor window.
* **Auto-Repair Protocol:** Automatically restores your equipment durability at any capable blacksmith or merchant using your character's gold.
* **Rapid Quest Progression:** Automatically accepts new quests and turns in completed ones, instantly pausing if a crucial reward choice is required.
* **Accelerated Loot Processing:** Dictates a force-empty routine on game loot windows, resulting in blindingly fast looting speeds.
* **Cinematic Skipper:** Instantly intercepts and cancels in-game cutscenes and movie cinematics so you can stay in action.

### 🎨 Next-Gen Visual Theming
* **Real-Time Theme Engine:** Switch your entire interface design fluidly between **Neon Cyan**, **Matrix Green**, **Oblivion Orange**, or **Minimalist Dark** with no reloads required.
* **Unified Tooltip Skinning:** Transforms every tooltip in the entire game into a gorgeous, translucent (0.82 alpha) HUD window with custom rounded corners and an inverted header glow.
* **Smart Valuation Tracker:** Injects concise single-item and full-stack vendor pricing info cleanly below standard item stats.
* **Dynamic Profile Coloration:** Automatically detects your character's class and displays your active profile banner in its official class color hex code.
* **Stepless Scale Control:** Features a live steepless control slider to dynamically scale all game tooltips between 80% and 120% on the fly.
